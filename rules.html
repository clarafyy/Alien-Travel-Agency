United Federation of Galactic Travel Guidelines

Effective Stardate: 9382.5.1

SECTION I: ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

All travelers must obtain a Cloaking Clearance Code (CCC) before atmospheric entry. Naked landings will result in planetary panic and fines up to ∅400,000.
Disguise Protocols are mandatory. Choose from any available human avatars as of update 10.33.101.
Use designated Arrival Zones. Initial entry into unapproved landing zones is strictly prohibited.

SECTION II: CULTURAL INTERACTION

As of 13002002, probing is illegal in all parts of the Milky Way galaxy.
